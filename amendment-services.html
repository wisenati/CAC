Amendment Services

Make changes to your registered business information, update company details, and file necessary amendments with the Corporate Affairs Commission

🏢 Business Name Amendments
For business names, the following amendment services are available. Filing of notice of change of business name is ₦5,000, while other amendments like address or proprietorship changes are ₦1,000 each.
Service Fee (₦) Actions
Change of Business Name ₦5,000
Change of Business Address ₦1,000
Change of Proprietorship ₦1,000
Filing of Annual Return ₦1,000
Filing of Notice of Cessation of Business ₦1,000
Filing of Other Documents (Miscellaneous) ₦1,000
Certified True Copy of Business Name Certificate ₦5,000
🏛️ Incorporated Trustees Amendments
For incorporated trustees, the following amendment services are available. Filing of notice of change of name or trustees is ₦10,000 each, while other amendments like constitution changes are ₦6,000.
Service Fee (₦) Actions
Change of Name ₦10,000
Change of Trustees ₦10,000
Amendment of Constitution ₦6,000
Filing of Annual Return ₦5,000
Filing of Notice of Court Order for Dissolution ₦5,000
Filing of Other Documents (Miscellaneous) ₦1,000
Certified True Copy of Incorporation Certificate ₦10,000
